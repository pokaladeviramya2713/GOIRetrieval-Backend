Title: Faculty Development Program

Category: Program

Department: Department of Higher Education

Description:
This initiative is launched by the Government of India to strengthen the higher education ecosystem in India. The program focuses on supporting students, promoting research and innovation, and improving the quality of higher education institutions through funding, policy development, and digital learning infrastructure.

Eligibility:
- Must meet minimum academic criteria
- Income should be within government limits

Benefits:
- Access to digital learning platforms
- Support for tuition fees
